How to play GALLOP!

  1. Read the field. Each horse shows speed, stamina, age and a weather preference. Stamina and form change every race.
  2. Mind the weather. It's revealed before you bet and favours horses that love those conditions.
  3. Place one bet. Pick a horse, a bet type, and a stake — then lock it in.
  4. Bet types. Win = 1st (full odds). Place = top 2 (¼ odds). Show = top 3 (⅕ odds).
  5. Survive. The game ends when your balance hits $0. Chase the highest balance you can reach.

No real money — just bragging rights. Good luck out there.
